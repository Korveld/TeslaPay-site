Questions?

We have answers!

What is TeslaPay?

TeslaPay is a mobile bank operating under a license from TESLAPAY LCC. This means that all your funds are fully secure on all TeslaPay accounts and cards.

What features does TeslaPay offer?

With TeslaPay, you can open accounts in USD, PLN and EUR. You can link up to 10 cards – virtual and plastic – to each account. For each card, you can choose a payment system – we offer Visa or MasterCard.

In which countries can TeslaPay be used?

You can pay for purchases with TeslaPay and withdraw cash from ATMs worldwide. Accounts can be opened by choosing IBAN from Switzerland, Lithuania, Germany, France (excluding French territories outside of Europe), Luxembourg, and others. To open an account, it is important to meet the requirements and procedures of KYC, which we apply when working with clients.

Is it safe to use TeslaPay?

Absolutely safe. Before opening an account, we conduct identification and KYC procedures for each client. All transactions are automatically analyzed for fraud. All cards we issue support 3D-secure technology. Furthermore, we recommend our clients use two-factor authentication and biometric authorization.

Why is user verification needed in the app?

We are fully committed to safe and transparent financial activity, so we request personal data to verify user identity, protect our users from fraud, and ensure the security of the services we provide. In some cases, we may request additional information.

How does TeslaPay store and process my personal data?

Data protection is our top priority. The personal data of our users and any other documents that users send us are securely stored in accordance with our 'Client Confidentiality Policy.'

How long does it take to open a bank account?

Normally, we open an account within a few hours. In rare cases, this may take up to several days.

When will I be able to receive a card for payment?

If you are issuing a virtual card, its opening is instant - you can add it to your mobile phone wallet and start using it. The issuance and delivery of a plastic card depend on the country and city of delivery. You will be able to find the terms of issuance and delivery date when you apply for a plastic card in the mobile application.
